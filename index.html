Our Story

Crafted with Passion.
Served with Fire.

Ember & Flame was born in 2018 from a singular obsession: to create the most memorable dining experience in Pakistan. What began as a 32-seat intimate steakhouse in F-6, Islamabad, has grown into the city's most celebrated fine dining destination.

Our Executive Chef, Zafar Mirza — trained at Le Cordon Bleu Paris and with a decade at Michelin-starred restaurants in London and Dubai — leads a team of passionate culinary artists who believe that food is the highest form of art.

"We don't just cook food. We craft memories. Every plate that leaves our kitchen is a story — of the farmer who grew it, the chef who dreamed it, and the guest who deserves it."

— Chef Zafar Mirza, Executive Chef & Co-Founder
